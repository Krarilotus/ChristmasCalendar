In extreme crisis, extreme measures have to be taken. Door 19 features an extreme scenario: you are stuck with wood and weapon production. That’s it.

Everyone else is against you, and even lions have taken over farmland. Secure your castle and win before your enemies outscale you or kill too many income buildings. Creativity and thinking outside the box help a lot.

Hint 1 Rush Richard. Otherwise he will bombard you with stone. You probably know how this works from previous maps.
Hint 2 Apothecaries are your safest bet. Healer stations wherever horse archers and troops need healing greatly improve cost efficiency.
Hint 3 You can produce and sell weapons. North of Richard are 3 iron mines, and near the river crossing is one hop farm to hold if using a resource-heavy approach.
Hint 4 Two ways to win: bunker safely and slowly build an army, or more interestingly, control as much of the map as possible at all times.
Hint 5 Stay out of Phillip’s mangonel range if you do not want to randomly lose troops.
Hint 6 Killing the lions early frees the deer behind your castle for hunting.
Hint 7 Horse archer micro kills most archers without taking many shots, but fire ballistae hit them faster. Watch out.
Hint 8 Use your troops constantly to control incoming attacks.
Hint 9 Enough slingers on walls and towers can handle assassins climbing gates.
Hint 10 Keep sortie units around to deal with siege engines early.
Hint 11 If you need resources, delete buildings you need less.
Hint 12 Clearing Caliph and Snake first with horse archers makes survival easier, as they rebuild defenses instead of attacking repeatedly.
