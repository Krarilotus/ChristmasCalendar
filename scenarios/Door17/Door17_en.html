You conquered the land back from the fanatics and entered the 17th portal, only to realize you have no clue where you are.

Bottom left sits Rat on valuable resources. In the top right waits the lord of this realm: Snake. Between them are five more Rats, ready to follow orders and throw everything at you.

The labyrinth is designed to confuse everyone. You and the AIs will struggle to navigate cliffs and walls. Can you conquer the land? Side note: your ranged unit options are reduced to slingers.

Hint 1 Slingers near an enemy keep can lure out the lord if you have no stronger melee units nearby.
Hint 2 Stack slingers when fighting a lord; cheap units can kill him efficiently.
Hint 3 If slingers are not enough, use your lord. He has more health. Later you will have knights as well.
Hint 4 You can block AI pathing to your area with walls inside the labyrinth.
Hint 5 Control the entry point the AI must use and stack slingers there. They are efficient against spearmen and archers.
Hint 6 Idle peasants at the campfire may not suit this map; you likely have enough gold to recruit cheap units anytime.
Hint 7 Rushing Rat gives you safe expansion space.
Hint 8 Move slingers into their preferred range against archers to win fights.
Hint 9 Burning enemy castles is possible, but destroying spending buildings and rushing the lord for his treasury can be better.
Hint 10 Heal your lord at the apothecary after securing a kill with him.
Hint 11 You can connect the bottom and side iron cliffs with stairs. One staircase is preinstalled; erase it if enemies abuse it, rebuild it later.
