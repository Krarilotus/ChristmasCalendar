Behind the curtains of the second door lies and invitation to play a match against the Sheriff. You plan your strategy ahead, looking at the grand overview of this tiny land.

You've established the bare minimum of an economy already. Two Sultans are towering above you on either side. A corridor through the mountains leads to the Sheriff’s castle, which is also the only way for enemies to approach your position. If I was you, I would hurry up, as they seem ready to strike any moment now. But who am I to tell, I only created this mess. Now its your turn to clean it up! Do you have what it takes?

I might have figured out some basic strategic hints which would give you an edge. I am sure you
won’t need them, but just in case, here they are:

Hint 1 Recruiting a basic defense right away with your starting weapons will give you enough time to build up an economy later on.
Hint 2 Sealing off the corridor with a wall or a moat is very cheap.
Hint 3 The farmland has more space then you might think. After all, bread production is very lucrative.
Hint 4 Harassing your enemies’ economy will keep them at bay.
Hint 5 Your enemies units seem to suffer a weakness against crossbow bolts.

Well equipped, you march into the field. For power and glory!
Have fun and good luck.
