Once upon a time... wait, what? We are here for door 12 of the calendar.

Here you go, playing like a snake. In this scenario you will face two other Snakes. The hill to your left is claimed by the orange Snake, with a typical S-shaped castle. To your right is another hill, claimed by the yellow Snake. His castle is formed by two separate snake-shaped walls, protected by archers on walls and towers.

If you do not act smart, you will soon struggle to keep up with their archer production. Here are some clues to withstand the pressure:

Hint 1 You will lose if you fight on all fronts at once. Find the magical spot where you can protect yourself without taking too many losses.
Hint 2 You might not have noticed it, but you have some slaves up your sleeve. They are currently running towards the orange Snake’s quarries.
Hint 3 You have an assassin, which you can use to kill crucial parts of the orange Snake’s economy.
Hint 4 After reducing both Snakes’ economies, make sure you have enough weapon production to keep up with their archer production.
Hint 5 You can use 5-10 catapults to decimate one Snake and gain the upper hand on one side. That makes nearby resources much easier to take.
