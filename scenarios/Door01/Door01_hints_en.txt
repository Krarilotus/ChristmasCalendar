If you need reinforcements to target a specific enemy or you are running short on supplies, Emir might be the one to ask for it.
For a small population, the maximum positive fear factor fighting bonus is already achieved by building 5 good things.
Engineers don't have to die with their manned siege engine and can be reused.
Fire throwers can cause hefty damage. I'd say either avoid staying on low ground in their range or kill them before marching at a castle.
Taking control over enemy defense structures with your own troops by climbing their staircases will give you an advantage while taking it from them at the same time.
Mangonels and trebuchets are great tools for sieging big castles. Trebuchets can outrange ballista towers by target firing with attack command in front of them.
Killing Lords will increase your army limit to take on stronger enemies.
